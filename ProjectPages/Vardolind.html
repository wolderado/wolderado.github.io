Vardolind

Nov 2016

Project Group Size: Solo

Development Time: 3 days

A First-Person atmospheric story game of mystery. Made for Asylum Game Jam 2016 with the theme "Escape Room". The Player has to find his son but there is a creature that can shape-shift. There are 5 different endings. It won the 23rd place out of 100 games. It had a really good reception from various youtubers.

Highlight Of The Project

Story based game systems
