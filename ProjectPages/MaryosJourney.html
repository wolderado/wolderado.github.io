Maryo's Journey

Mar 2018

Project Group Size: 2

Development Time: 1 Week

The concept of the jam was to create a meta game. We've made a platformer that the character is aware of the player. The game is full of comedic dialogue and 4th wall breaking moments. Game has pixel-perfect camera but it can be improved. You can try the game out on your browser right now
